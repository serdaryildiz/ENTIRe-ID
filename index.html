ENTIRe-ID: An Extensive and Diverse Dataset for Person Re-Identification

Serdar Yıldız1,3, Ahmet Nezih Kasım2,3
1Yıldız Technical University, 2Bogazici University, 3BİLGEM, TÜBİTAK
Paper Code Data

Abstract

The growing importance of person re-identification in computer vision has highlighted the need for more extensive and diverse datasets. In response, we introduce the ENTIRe-ID dataset, an extensive collection comprising over 4.45 million images from 37 different cameras in varied environments. This dataset is uniquely designed to tackle the challenges of domain variability and model generalization, areas where existing datasets for person re-identification have fallen short. The ENTIRe-ID dataset stands out for its coverage of a wide array of real-world scenarios, encompassing various lighting conditions, angles of view, and diverse human activities. This design ensures a realistic and robust training platform for ReID models.
